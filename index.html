About Us

Weaving and printing,
under one roof.

For four decades, Firma Textile has drawn, woven, and roller-printed fabric in a single mill — patterns developed in-house, colour matched by eye, finished by hand.

We supply apparel manufacturers and fashion houses with custom designs, repeatable colourways, and runs scaled to your order — one point of contact, from first swatch to finished bolt.
